About Us

Our Mission

To stand up for justice, choice, and equality. To champion the rights of women and children and other oppressed groups. To transform discrimination into understanding, justice, and equality.

Our Vision for the Community

Poverty, patriarchy, exploitation, and discrimination will no longer exist. Equality, respect, and inclusion will prevail. The ideals of feminism will be the norm. Community and provincial systems and institutions will be dedicated to practicing true justice and equality.

Our Goals

  • Provide one-on-one advocacy that offers information, support, and representation in a non-judgmental setting encouraging self-advocacy.
  • Provide a voice for the voiceless in our community and province, particularly in situations of power imbalance.
  • Make issues of poverty, violence against women, mental illness, and child abuse visible in our community and beyond.
  • Encourage all members of our community to work towards preventing and solving societal problems.
  • Ensure everyone in our community is aware of their rights and has access to justice and fair treatment.
  • Change unfair legislation, policies, and practices at national, provincial, and local levels.
  • Foster a safe, productive, and creative workspace by taking care of ourselves and each other.
